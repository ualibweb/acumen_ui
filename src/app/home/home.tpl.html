What's in Acumen?

Acumen contains over 1600 finding aids and 90,000 digitized items, ranging from manuscripts and photographs to audio, sheet music, and student dissertations. Here you will find a wealth of information about the history of Alabama and the Deep South, as well as the United States more generally. The digitized collections span two centuries and cover a range of topics, including business and industry, agriculture, politics and war, education, and daily life.
